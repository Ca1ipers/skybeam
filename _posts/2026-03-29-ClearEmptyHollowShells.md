---
title: Clear, Empty, Hollow Shells
layout: post
---
There are clear skies outside. <br>
There are no clouds to shroud the setting sun. <br>
There are whistles of gentle winds that sway the fields. <br>
There is nobody to watch. <br>
There is nothing to see.

There are empty streets and sidewalks. <br>
There are no cars to fill the city with noise. <br>
There are rays of sunshine scattered across the cityscape. <br>
There is nobody to impress. <br>
There is nothing to prove.

There are hollow hallways in this building. <br>
There are no distractions or decorations. <br>
There are hums of the machinery behind every door. <br>
There is nobody to hear. <br>
There is nothing to do.

There are shells of clear waters that extend deep into darkness. <br>
There are shells of empty promises and thoughts. <br>
There are shells of hollow points scattered across the floor. <br>
There is a shell of body bags and blood. <br>
There is a shell of what I used to be.
